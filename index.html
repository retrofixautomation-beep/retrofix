Case Study

Proven Results, Guaranteed Uptime.

Challenge: At Taluk Hospital Chalakkudy, Thrissur, a critical Tuttnauer disinfector washer was down for over a year. Even the OEM was unable to repair it and suggested a high-cost board replacement.

Solution: Retrofix Automation took on the challenge where others failed. We performed component-level diagnostics on the existing control board.

Result: We restored the machine to full function within 4 days without the expensive board replacement, saving the hospital significant costs and downtime.
